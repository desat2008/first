Jello Clothing

Introduction

Jello Clothing is a contemporary, direct-to-consumer apparel brand founded in Sri Lanka, distinguished by its dedication to comfort, quality, and vibrant colour. Emerging as a modern force in the local fashion landscape, Jello has successfully differentiated itself by focusing on elevated basics and essentials, appealing directly to a customer base that prioritizes both local authenticity and personal style.

History

Jello Clothing is a recent direct-to-consumer brand that filled a gap in the Sri Lankan market by offering high-quality, minimalist basics in diverse colours. Its success is driven by a strong online presence, appealing to younger customers who value authenticity and local craftsmanship. Jello's identity is founded on being "Designed & Manufactured in Sri Lanka."

Future

Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most loved everyday-wear brand, inspiring individuals to express their unique personalities through a spectrum of colour, without ever compromising on comfort or quality. Jello envisions a world where clothing is a source of simple, daily joy and confidence.

Mission

Jello Clothing's mission is to design, manufacture, and deliver exceptionally comfortable and versatile apparel in a wide array of appealing colours. They are committe to upholding high standards of quality by keeping their entire production process within Sri Lanka, thereby supporting local industry and ensuring meticulous craftsmanship in every piece they create.

Values

Jello operates on a set of clear and impactful values that are reflected in their products and communication.
"Comfort is Key","Vibrant Self-Expression","Inclusivity and Diversity","Local Pride and Quality","Simplicity and Timelessness"
